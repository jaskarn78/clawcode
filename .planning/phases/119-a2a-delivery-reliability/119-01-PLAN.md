<objective>
Land the bot-direct fallback rung in `post_to_agent` (A2A-01) and wire WebhookManager 401/404 cache invalidation + one-shot reprovision (A2A-02) in the same module/wave/deploy. Mirror the canonical Phase 999.12 IPC-02 shape from `daemon-ask-agent-ipc.ts:262-299` verbatim (D-01). Both A2A-01 and A2A-02 touch the live cross-agent delivery surface and share a single deploy/integration cycle — bundling them keeps the boot-sentinel observation window aligned with both fixes.

Per D-02 the boot-time sentinel is the single source of truth for "is the new fallback wired in production." Verification artifact reads journalctl for `[A2A-01-sentinel] OK` — pre-committed log keyword, not generic success grep (silent-path-bifurcation memory).

Per D-09 the existing `{ok, delivered, messageId, reason?}` shape stays; the new rung sets `delivered: true` on bot-direct success and falls through to `inboxOnlyResponse(...)` on failure. No IPC contract change.

Output:
- `handlePostToAgentIpc` accepts optional `botDirectSender` + `agentChannels` deps (same shape as `daemon-ask-agent-ipc.ts`).
- New bot-direct rung inserted between the existing `no-webhook` skip (line 193-199) and the `inboxOnlyResponse` return (line 198) — when webhook is absent AND a channel is bound, attempt bot-direct text send; on success return `{ok:true, delivered:true, messageId}`; on failure log warning and fall through to inbox.
- Structured pino info log `{agent: to, channel, reason}` on every bot-direct attempt (A2A-01 operator observability requirement).
- WebhookManager `sendAsAgent` catch branch: on `error.code === 401 || error.code === 404` (DiscordAPIError shape), delete cached entry, call bot to provision fresh webhook, retry the send ONCE with the new webhook; if retry also fails, surface failure normally.
- Daemon-ready hook fires synthetic `admin→admin` post_to_agent and asserts `{delivered: true}`; logs `[A2A-01-sentinel] OK` on success, `[A2A-01-sentinel] FAIL` + structured warning on failure.
- Daemon's `post-to-agent` IPC case body (`daemon.ts:8491-8492` region) is updated to pass the new `botDirectSender` + `agentChannels` deps to `handlePostToAgentIpc`.
</objective>
