# Phase 125 Plan 03 — Tier 2 Structured Haiku Extraction

## Goal

Add Tier 2 to the pipeline established in Plan 02: a Haiku-driven structured extractor that reads the `toCompact` text and emits a YAML/JSON structured block covering active client names, decisions made, standing-rule changes, in-flight tasks, drive paths, and recover-or-lose-it numbers. Persist the output to BOTH `~/.clawcode/agents/<agent>/state/active-state.yaml` (durable read for active-state header refresh) AND `memory.db` as chunks (RRF-recallable across full reset). Reuses the proven `src/memory/session-summarizer.ts` Haiku-call shape (D-03) — do NOT invent a fresh worker.
